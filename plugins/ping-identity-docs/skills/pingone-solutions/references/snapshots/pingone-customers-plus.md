# About PingOne for Customers Plus

The PingOne for Customers Plus solution lets you offer your end users a variety of flexible experiences for account creation, sign-on, profile management, and account recovery using a simple getting started experience and pre-built DaVinci flows.

You can view additional information about the solution, including download links, in the marketplace. Learn more at [PingOne for Customers Plus on the Marketplace](https://marketplace.pingone.com/item/pingone-for-customers-plus).

Preparing the PingOne for Customers Plus solution involves configuration steps in PingOne and DaVinci.

* In PingOne, perform basic configuration to prepare the flows to be launched and enable features such as MFA and PingOne notifications.

* In DaVinci, configure variables and flow settings to customize the flow behavior for your environment and customers.

* Clone the flows for use in your production environment.

The PingOne for Customers Plus solution offers many of the available DaVinci capabilities. However, the solution limits the pre-built components to common use cases and selected best practices.

The solution includes a variety of authentication methods, such as email magic link, email and SMS OTP, FIDO2, voice, mobile applications, and Time-based One-Time Password (TOTP) *(tooltip: \<div class="paragraph">
\<p>A temporary passcode generated by an algorithm that uses the current time of day as one of its authentication factors. Typically, an app or hardware token generates a 6-digit passcode that is valid for less than 1 minute.\</p>
\</div>)*.
